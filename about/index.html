Who is Stephen

Stephen is a software developer at Intel. He works on OpenStack, with a focus on NFV enablement. In the past, he has also contributed Intel’s DPDK-focused fork of Open vSwitch - DPDK vSwitch - along with Open vSwitch itself. He hold an first class honors degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Limerick, Ireland.
