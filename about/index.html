Who is Stephen

Stephen is a software developer at Red Hat. He works on OpenStack, with a focus on NFV enablement. He previously worked for Intel, where he worked on OpenStack and contributed to both Intel’s DPDK-focused fork of Open vSwitch - DPDK vSwitch - along with Open vSwitch itself. He hold an first class honors degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Limerick, Ireland.
