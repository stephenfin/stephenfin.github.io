Stephen is a senior software engineer at Red Hat. He’s been heavily involved in the OpenStack community since 2015, including related projects across the Python ecosystem. He’s also the maintainer of Patchwork, the patch tracking tool used by the kernel and many other FOSS communities. He holds a BEng in Compute Engineering from the University of Limerick and has a keen interest in virtualisation, Platform-as-a-Service (PaaS) and Infrastructure-as-a-Service (IaaS).
